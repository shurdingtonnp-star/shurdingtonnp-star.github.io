Who Are We?

We are a volunteer group dedicated to creating a future vision for Shurdington community. Our role is to create a set of policies that will influence and guide all future planning and development in Shurdington. We are engaging with residents, developers, and many stakeholders to shape the future of our local area. We would love you to be involved too, please contact us if you have opinions or ideas about the future of your community.

How the NDP Process Works

The NDP process is designed to be transparent, efficient, and inclusive. We engage our community throughout each stage. We welcome your ideas and input.

1

Setting up the NDP Group.

The NDP group was formed in early 2025. You are welcome to join the NDP Working Group. Please contact us.

2

Defining our Community Area.

Defining and agreeing what is considered as being within Shurdington, and why.

3

Public Consultation

Community engagement period allowing residents and stakeholders to review and comment on initial proposals.

4

Assessment

Technical review by planning experts and legal advisors ensuring policy compliance and appropriate community feedback.

5

Decision

Once the examiner and principal authority have agreed then the The NDP is put to the electorate and a decision is made. If succesful, the NDP becomes legally binding.

6

Implementation

Seeking to ensure that the policies the community have chosen and defined are being implemented by developers and other community and government organisations.

Our Chosen Local Planning Policies.

These are the key planning policies that the Shurdington NDP group consider to be the most important for our community.

Six Current Policy Areas

These are the key planning policies that the Shurdington NDP group consider to be the most important for our community..

Housing Development Policy

Guidelines for residential development including affordable housing requirements, density standards, and design principles to create sustainable communities.

Last Updated: December 2025

Environmental Protection Policy

Requirements for development to recognise environmental impact, protect biodiversity, and incorporate sustainable development practices in all planning applications.

Last Updated: August 2025

Transport and Infrastructure Policy

Ensuring that road safety (including excessive speeding), parking provision, public transport accessibility, and connecting our community are incorporated into planning decsions..

Last Updated: September 2025

Design and Heritage Policy

Ensuring that the importance of the Cotswold National Landscape is recognised by new development. Respecting our heritage and conservation requirements and maintaining local character.

Last Updated: June 2025

Development Inclusivity and Enabling Opportunities Policy

Ensuring that all development presents equal opportunities for all elements of our community whether private, commercial or government.

Last Updated: September 2025

Community Facilities Policy

Recognising requirements for appropriate community infrastructure including schools, healthcare, recreation facilities, and developer contributions.

Last Updated: August 2025
